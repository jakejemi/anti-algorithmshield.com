Privacy Policy

Anti-Algorithm Shield is designed to protect user privacy and reduce tracking and profiling.

Data Collection

Anti-Algorithm Shield does not sell or share personal data. The app does not require user accounts.

Network Processing

When enabled, Anti-Algorithm Shield processes network traffic locally or through an encrypted connection for the sole purpose of privacy protection.

Third-Party Sharing

No user data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Permissions

On Android, optional permissions may be requested to provide usage insights and security reporting. These permissions are used only for app functionality.
